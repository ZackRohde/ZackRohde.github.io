About Me

2012 - 2014

I graduated from high school and enlisted into the Marine Corps. I waited 7 months for an infantry contract before I left for boot camp Jan 2013. I was stationed at Camp Pendleton for my entire enlistment. I deployed my first time in 2013-2014 on a 6 month deployment to Austrailia were we trained with the Australian Army and New Zeland Army creating rapport and setting up what is now a very large forwarding operating base.

2015 - 2016

My 2nd deployment came in 2015-2016 where we were on the 31st MEU (Marine Expedtionary Unit) and I was assigned the role squad leader where I had around 10 Marines under my watch. We sailed the North Pacfic Ocean visting South Korea, Japan, Okiniawa, and Thailand.

2016 - 2017

Once it became closer to me getting out around the end of 2016, I had the opportunity to join PMO (Provost Marshall's Office, also known as Military Police) where I would stay over 6 months. I quickly excelled and went from gate guard to working on the streets responding to 911 calls. As my time came to an end I looked forward to what the civilian world had to offer and March 14th, 2017 I offically became a veteran of the Marine Corps.

2017

Fresh out of the military I start my civilian journey. Fitness has always been a passion of mine as I believe health and fitness is the way to live a successful life. I joined 24 hour fitness as a personal trainer in Oceanside, CA where I had many clients who were either looking to gain weight and strength or other clients looking to lose a few pounds. I stayed on this adventure for around 6 months until I started realizing peoeple will only help themselves if they truly want a better life and sad to say majority of the time they took the easy way out. I knew health and fitness was for me, but personal training was not at that time.

2017 - 2020

I also started working on my higher education in 2017 where I attended National University where I would graudate with a Bachelors Degree in Management. I focused on school very much through 2017 and 2018 until I was ready to enter the workforce again. End of 2018 I got hired as a Front Desk Associate at Eos Fitness in Temecula, CA. 4 months of being there I got promoted to Assistant Operations Manager to Operations Manager at a bigger gym location. I excelled and loved every minute of it until March 2020 where we had the COVID-29 pandemic and I was furloughed..

2020 - 2022

I knew I had to do something in order to support myself and find a career that wouldn't be put on hold when a major circumstance happens. I found myself studying to become an MLO (Mortgage Loan Originator) where I found a passion helping veterans and active duty military members use their VA home loan benefit to own a home they fought for. I absoultely loved working for Aligned Mortgage until May of 2022 where I was laid off due to the down market. I was once again looking for what to do..

2022 - 2023

I discovered what coding really is. I never understand what it was or how it worked, but it's always peaked my interest as I was jealous that I couldn't do what software engineer's did. That turned into a reality when I joined Learn Acdemy Sept 2022 for a 4 month coding bootcamp where we learned JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Postrgresql, Git, and GithHub. Jan 13th is my expected date and I look forward to what the future holds.
